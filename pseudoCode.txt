Pseudo Code

1) Four crystals will be displayed on the player's screen
2) A random generated number is shown on the player's screen
3) The player has to click on one of the crystals till it adds up to the number shown on the player's screen.
4) The random number generated for the crystals will be hidden.
5) If the player's number goes above the target they will get a loss.
6) If it adds up perfectly the player will win
7) All the random numbers will reset after a win or loss.
